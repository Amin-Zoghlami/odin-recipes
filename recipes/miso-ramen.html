Miso Ramen

Description

Ichiraku's miso ramen was Naruto Uzumaki's favorite meal thoughout the anime. Now you can learn how to make one of Naruto's and my favorite dishes, miso ramen, on your own. This page will go over how to approach each of the three components of ramen. Those being the soup, noodles, and toppings.

Ingredients

  • Miso
  • Bean Paste
  • Sesame Seeds or Oil
  • Chicken Broth
  • White Pepper Powder
  • Fresh or Dried Noodles
  • Pork Belly
  • Egg
  • Green Onion
  • Narutomaki
