Burger

Description

Burgers are one of the most popular foods here in the United States. I have eaten plenty of burgers from both high level resturants and fast-food resturants. Here, you will learn how you can make your own burger in one of my favorite ways.

Ingredients

  • Oil
  • Egg
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Ground Beef
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Buns
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard (personal choice)

Steps

  1. Preheat grill at high heat with light oil
  2. Mix egg, salt and pepper in a bowl
  3. Add ground beef and bread crumbs and mix until blended
  4. Form into patties (3/4-inch-thick)
  5. Place patties on the grill for around 6-8 minutes (patties should be fully cooked)
  6. (Optional) Grill buns for a few seconds to toast
  7. Put desired sauces on the buns, then place the patty, tomato, onions, and lettuce between the buns
  8. Enjoy your meal!
